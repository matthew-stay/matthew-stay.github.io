The Overstay

Identity

The Overstay is an art space, venue, and backpacker hostel in Bangkok, Thailand. They wanted to refresh their identity to highlight the community environemnt their hostel encourages. We stayed in Bangkok for a month to develop the new logo, along with posters, tshirts, and a basic identity manual.
