Sight Reading Factory

App, Mobile, Creative Direction

Sight reading is a skill musicians use for looking at a new piece of music for the first time and being able to play it immediately. Sight Reading Factory has been working on making it easier to practice sight reading with an interactive webapp. We worked with them to develop a completely new native IOS experience.

Details

Sight reading is a skill musicians use for looking at a new piece of music for the first and being able to play it immediately. This is a tough task as your learning - its difficult to pick up a song and hope you can already play everything on it.

Clear Content

This project involved organizing and pruning the content from an aging site. Extraneous language was removed to get to the core of what was important to say for the brand. Once this was complete, the new major site structure could be easily defined by the content.

Wireframe

Basic page sections are mocked up, further organizing our content and clarifying the focus of each page.

Calls to Action

Calls to Action are one of the common elements found on the end of multiple pages. Users can make up their mind during any point of their experience to take action. We can also track which page has the most impact, easily tracking engagement.
