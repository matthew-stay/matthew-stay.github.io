Watscroll Card Template

Identity

Designed to fit a standard 2.5"x3.5" card sleeve, this warscroll card template is flexible enough to fit many of the models in Warhammer: Age of Sigmar.

Details

To use this template, simply download the psd, open in photoshop and follow along with the instructions. Once your done, you can print, cut, and fold the cards. You'll want some kind of card sleeve to keep these in so everything stays secure.

If you enjoy the template, please share it. If you have feedback or other ideas, let me know!
